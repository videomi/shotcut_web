<ul><li>Hidden</li><li>In Only - Large</li><li>In Only - Small (default)</li><li>In and Out - Left/Right (shown above)</li><li>In and Out - Top/Bottom (vertically stacked <i>in</i> above <i>out</i>)</li></ul>
